Gothic Lockpick Solver

Browser-based solver for Gothic 1 Remake locks.

Solve any lock locally

Enter plate positions, map how plates affect each other, and calculate the shortest safe solution directly in the browser.

Direction labels in the final solution are based on the key you press in game, so they are inverted compared to the visual movement of the plate.

Lock setup

Initial positions

Set each plate from hole 1 to 7. Hole 4 is the target center.

Plate links
